Our Journey

From a Small IT
Firm to East Africa's
Trusted Integrator

Navantis Systems was founded in 2010 in Kampala, Uganda, with a clear mission: to make world-class ICT infrastructure accessible to African businesses. What began as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a full-service technology solutions company serving clients across Uganda and the broader East African region.

Today, we offer eight specialised service domains — from biometric access control and IP surveillance to enterprise wireless networks and custom application development. Our certified engineers bring both technical depth and practical African market experience to every project.
